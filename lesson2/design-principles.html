Hick's Law

Tesla

Tesla's homepage is a great example of Hick's law. The objective of Hick's law is to simplify the decision-making process. The page presents two choices that stick to the mission which is to sell cars to users: either making a custom order and searching existing inventory.

Visual Hierarchy

WS

Williams-Sonoma.com's home page demonsrates the principle of Visual Hierarchy in web design. This pages promote pans so the biggest content is the pictures of the pans, followed by the headline that tells potential customers what it is, and a call to shop these pans comes third.

White Space & Clean Design

Tesla

Tesla's homepage presents a very simple and clean design with good potion of white space. The clean design makes targeted customers focus on the misson that the company want them to focus: buying a car!
