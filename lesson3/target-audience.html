Target Audience

The target audience of the forecastnow.us website is described below:
* Locals who want to get weather information for their outdoor activies.
* Leisure and business travellers to the Pacific Northwest area of the US, as well as locals who want to get updated and accurate weather information.

Scenarios

1. Is the weather good and safe for me and my friends to hike to Mountain Si tomorrow?
2. Do I need to pack an extra layer for the hike or shall I bring a lot water for hot weather?
3. Is there any snow for my kids to play next week?
4. Do I need to bring rain jacket for my trip to Seattle in three days?
5. I am going to our Seattle office for an outdoor event. Is it going to rain on that day? Maybe I should bring an umbrella so my suit doesn't get wet.
6. I am visiting Mount Rainer with my family next week and is there a relative warmer day for us to go?
