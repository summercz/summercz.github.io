Our Misson

Changshu Chamber of Commerce dedicates to promote businesses and commerce in Changshu, Jiangsu Province of China, a historic yet viberant city nesting in the lower reaches of the Yangtze River. People in Changshu have had a long history engaging in active business and commercial activities. Its adjacent to big international cities like Suzhou and Shanghai makes Changshu in a unique place to balance Development and keeping its own culture and history. Our mission is to connect business and people in Changshu with the world, as well as welcome and help people from all over the world to explore this beautiful city.
